Reiki Healing

Reiki is an ancient Japanese healing practice that channels universal life force energy - the energy we are born with that permeates everything. This healing treatment balances the body's energy system and helps you achieve balance physically, mentally, and emotionally.

We begin with a conversation where we discuss what you need right now and what underlying issues may be present. Since the healing is performed at a distance, you choose a place where you can lie comfortably and be relaxed.

I connect to the universal energy and become a channel for it, allowing it to intuitively flow into your body to where it's needed right now. Many experience increased calm, less worry, and reduced physical pain.

The session ends with a conversation about any sensations you may have experienced. There is time and space for your soul to express itself. This is your time and there's nothing you need to deliver - you simply receive.

How Reiki Works

What to Expect

Preparation for Your Session

Come with an open mind and comfortable clothing. We'll create a peaceful, sacred space for your healing journey.

  • Wear comfortable, loose clothing
  • Eat lightly before the session
  • Drink plenty of water before and after the session
  • Set an intention for healing
  • Come with an open heart and mind

During Your Session

Since the healing is performed at a distance, you choose a comfortable place where you can lie down and relax. I connect to the universal energy and become a channel for it, allowing the healing energy to flow intuitively to where it's needed in your body. You may experience warmth, tingling, deep relaxation, increased calm, or reduced physical discomfort.

After Your Session

The session ends with a conversation about any sensations you may have experienced. There is time and space for your soul to express itself. Take time to rest and integrate the healing energy. Drink plenty of water, be gentle with yourself, and allow the healing process to continue naturally.
