Reiki Healing

Reiki is an ancient Japanese healing practice that channels universal life force energy to promote balance, healing, and well-being. Through gentle touch or hands placed just above the body, this peaceful practice supports your body's natural ability to heal itself.

During a Reiki session, you remain fully clothed while lying comfortably. The healing energy flows naturally to where it's needed most, bringing deep relaxation and a sense of peace and harmony.

Each session creates a sacred space for healing, allowing universal life force energy to flow freely and restore balance to mind, body, and spirit.

How Reiki Works

1. Energy Flow

Universal life force energy is channeled through the practitioner's hands to the recipient.

2. Natural Healing

The energy flows to where it's needed most, supporting the body's natural healing processes.

3. Balance & Peace

Deep relaxation and inner peace emerge as energy blockages are gently released.

What to Expect

Preparation for Your Session

Come with an open mind and comfortable clothing. We'll create a peaceful, sacred space for your healing journey.

  • Wear comfortable, loose clothing
  • Stay hydrated before and after
  • Set an intention for healing
  • Come with an open heart and mind

During Your Session

You'll remain fully clothed while lying comfortably on a treatment table. I'll place my hands gently on or just above your body, allowing healing energy to flow naturally. You may experience warmth, tingling, or deep relaxation.

After Your Session

Take time to rest and integrate the healing energy. Drink plenty of water, be gentle with yourself, and allow the healing process to continue naturally.
