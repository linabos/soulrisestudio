Remember Your Power

A three-month journey back to calm, presence and your own power.

A live online program in a small group, for you who long to slow down, remember who you are and come home to your power.

More calm. More presence. More spark and flow in body and life.

Over twelve weeks we meet online once a week, about 2 hours each time. Together we move through a gentle yet powerful journey in three phases: first to land in the body, then to open to what lies deeper, and finally to remember and step into your own power. Step by step you build a daily practice that actually sticks, because you don't do it alone and are held the whole way.

This isn't recorded material you watch in solitude. It's a living, safe space where you are seen and where change is given time to truly settle.

What's Included

  • 🤍 Twelve live sessions, once a week
  • 🤍 A different focus each time: meditation, breath or energy activation (Life Force Activation)
  • 🤍 Time for questions, sharing and connection
  • 🤍 Simple tools to weave into your everyday life
  • 🤍 Miss a Life Force Activation? You can make it up in the regular schedule

Who it's for

For you who long for more calm and presence, who want a community rather than yet another thing to do alone, and who are ready to give yourself three months of real care. No previous experience is needed.

  • 🗓️ Starts in September, twelve weeks
  • 💻 Online via Zoom, wherever you are
  • 👥 A small group

The Journey in Three Phases

Phase 1 · Land

Safety and ground. Coming home to the body and landing in a calm you can rest in.

Phase 2 · Open

Feeling and releasing. With safety in place, we dare to meet what lies deeper.

Phase 3 · Shine

Shine and live in flow. Letting your power become a natural part of everyday life.
