Home to Yourself

A three-month journey back to calm, presence and your own energy.

A live online program in a small group, for you who long to slow down and come home to yourself.

More calm. More presence. More spark and flow in body and life.

Over twelve weeks we meet online once a week, about 2.5 hours each time. Together we move through a gentle yet powerful journey in three phases: first to land in the body, then to open to what lies deeper, and finally to step into a new sense of calm and presence. Step by step you build a daily practice that actually sticks, because you don't do it alone and are held the whole way.

This isn't recorded material you watch in solitude. It's a living, safe space where you are seen and where change is given time to truly settle.

What's Included

  • 🤍 Twelve live online sessions via Zoom, once a week
  • 🤍 A small group of 6–8 people
  • 🤍 Guided practices each week with meditation, breath and energy work
  • 🤍 Your own daily practice to carry with you
  • 🤍 Recordings of the sessions if you ever miss one

Who it's for

For you who long for more calm and presence, who want a community rather than yet another thing to do alone, and who are ready to give yourself three months of real care. No previous experience is needed.

  • 🗓️ Starts in September, twelve weeks
  • 💻 Online via Zoom, wherever you are
  • 👥 Only 6–8 spots

The Journey in Three Phases

Phase 1 · Land

Safety and ground. Coming home to the body and building the foundation everything rests on.

Phase 2 · Open

Feeling and releasing. With safety in place, we dare to meet what lies deeper.

Phase 3 · Rise

Integrate and carry forward. Embodying the new and making it your own.
